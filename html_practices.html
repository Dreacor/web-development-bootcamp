This is H3

And this is H4

These are not used for the size of the text they generate, but rather definig the level of the header. These are part of the structure that should be logical: We don't use H3 before H2 for an example.

Another example: H1 could be called "Earth", H2 header could be something like "Continents" and under that we can have H3 called "Countries" and again under that we could have H4 called "Areas (inside the country)"
